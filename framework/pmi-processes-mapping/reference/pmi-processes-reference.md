### 4.1 Develop Project Charter (Initiating)

- **Inputs:** Business documents (Business case, Benefits management plan); Agreements; Enterprise environmental factors; Organizational process assets.
- **Tools & Techniques:** Expert judgment; Data gathering (Brainstorming, Focus groups, Interviews); Interpersonal and team skills (Conflict management, Facilitation, Meeting management); Meetings.
- **Outputs:** Project charter; Assumption log.
- **Data flow:** Feeds 4.2 Identify Stakeholders, 5.1 Develop Project Management Plan, and planning processes 5.2–5.24; receives from Enterprise/Organization, Project Documents; links to 8.1 Close Project or Phase.

---

### 4.2 Identify Stakeholders (Initiating)

- **Inputs:** Project charter; Business documents (Business case, Benefits management plan); Project management plan (Communications management plan, Stakeholder engagement plan); Project documents (Change log, Issue log, Requirements documentation); Agreements; Enterprise environmental factors; Organizational process assets.
- **Tools & Techniques:** Expert judgment; Data gathering (Questionnaires and surveys, Brainstorming); Data analysis (Stakeholder analysis, Document analysis); Data representation (Stakeholder mapping/representation); Meetings.
- **Outputs:** Stakeholder register; Change requests; Project management plan updates (Requirements management plan, Communications management plan, Risk management plan, Stakeholder engagement plan); Project documents updates (Assumption log, Issue log, Risk register).
- **Data flow:** Feeds 5.1, 5.17, 5.24, 6.7, 6.10, 7.9, 7.12; receives from 4.1, 7.2 Perform Integrated Change Control.

---

### 5.1 Develop Project Management Plan (Planning)

- **Inputs:** Project charter; Outputs from other processes; Enterprise environmental factors; Organizational process assets.
- **Tools & Techniques:** Expert judgment; Data gathering (Brainstorming, Checklists, Focus groups, Interviews); Interpersonal and team skills (Conflict management, Facilitation, Meeting management); Meetings.
- **Outputs:** Project management plan.
- **Data flow:** From 4.1 Develop Project Charter, Enterprise/Organization, outputs from other planning processes; produces integrated Project management plan.

---

### 5.2 Plan Scope Management (Planning)

- **Inputs:** Project charter; Project management plan (Quality management plan, Project life cycle description, Development approach); EEF; OPA.
- **Tools & Techniques:** Expert judgment; Data analysis (Alternatives analysis); Meetings.
- **Outputs:** Scope management plan; Requirements management plan.
- **Data flow:** From 4.1, Enterprise/Organization; outputs to PM Plan.

### 5.3 Collect Requirements (Planning)

- **Inputs:** Project charter; Project management plan (Scope management plan, Requirements management plan, Stakeholder engagement plan); Project documents (Assumption log, Lessons learned register, Stakeholder register); Business documents (Business case); Agreements; EEF; OPA.
- **Tools & Techniques:** Expert judgment; Data gathering (Brainstorming, Interviews, Focus groups, Questionnaires and surveys, Benchmarking); Data analysis (Document analysis); Decision making (Voting, Multicriteria decision analysis); Data representation (Affinity diagrams, Mind mapping); Interpersonal and team skills (Nominal group technique, Observation/conversation, Facilitation); Context diagram; Prototypes.
- **Outputs:** Requirements documentation; Requirements traceability matrix.
- **Data flow:** From 4.1, 6.9, PM plan, Project/Business documents; to Project Documents, PM Plan.

- **Tools & Techniques:** Data analysis (Alternatives, Root cause, Stakeholder analysis); Decision making (Multicriteria, Voting); Data representation (Stakeholder engagement assessment matrix); Communication skills (Feedback, Presentations); Interpersonal skills (Active listening, Cultural awareness, Leadership, Networking, Political awareness); Meetings.
