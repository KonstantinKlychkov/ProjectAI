# Mapping: Project Management Focus Areas to Performance Domains

Соответствие групп процессов PMI (Focus Areas) и Performance Domains по методологии Process Groups Practice Guide. Основа для распределения 49 процессов по доменам.

| Focus Area | Governance | Scope | Schedule | Finance | Stakeholders | Resources | Risk |
|------------|------------|-------|----------|---------|--------------|-----------|------|

| **Planning** | Develop Project Management Plan; integrate plans | Plan Scope Mgmt; Collect Requirements; Define Scope; Create WBS | Plan Schedule Mgmt; Define Activities; Sequence Activities; Estimate Durations; Develop Schedule | Plan Cost Mgmt; Estimate Costs; Determine Budget | Plan Stakeholder Engagement; Plan Communications Mgmt | Plan Resource Mgmt; Estimate Activity Resources | Plan Risk Mgmt; Identify Risks; Qualitative/Quantitative Analysis; Plan Risk Responses; Plan Procurement Mgmt |
| **Executing** | Direct and Manage Project Work | — | — | — | Manage Stakeholder Engagement; Manage Communications | Acquire Resources; Develop Team; Manage Team | Implement Risk Responses; Conduct Procurements |
| **Monitoring & Controlling** | Monitor and Control Project Work; Perform Integrated Change Control | Validate Scope; Control Scope | Control Schedule | Control Costs | Monitor Stakeholder Engagement; Monitor Communications | Control Resources | Monitor Risks; Control Procurements |
| **Closing** | Close Project or Phase | — | — | — | — | — | — |
